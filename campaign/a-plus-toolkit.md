# Amazon A+ Content — The Aging Parent Cardinal's Toolkit

Copy and art direction for the four A+ modules, built to the brand guide
(assets/brand-guide.html). Grounds: Paper #F7F2E9. Red: #B3222B. Headlines in
Spectral; labels in sans, uppercase, letterspaced. A+ modules are images —
these specs go to the designer with the marks in assets/marks/.

---

## Module 1 — Brand banner (full-width, Paper ground)

**Art:** Toolkit cover at left; the Perch mark small at right; red rule along
the bottom edge.

**Headline (Spectral, large):**
> The manual nobody hands you.

**Support line (sans):**
> Straight answers for Medicare, Medicaid, dementia, assisted living, and

## Module 2 — Three tiles (what's inside)

Three equal tiles on Paper, each opening with a red uppercase label and one
Toolbox-mark bullet style. No photography — clean type and the marks.

**Tile 1 — STRAIGHT ANSWERS**
> Medicare vs. Medicaid in plain English. The five-year rule. What hospice
> actually costs (usually nothing). 25 chapters in the order the road unfolds.

**Tile 2 — THE CARDINAL'S TOOLBOX**
> Fill-in worksheets for the moments you need one clear next step: fall log,
> medication list, doctor questions, facility tours, family meeting notes.

**Tile 3 — NORTH CAROLINA DIRECTORY**
> The agencies, programs, and phone numbers NC families actually use — so
> you're not calling national hotlines for county problems.

## Module 3 — Comparison (the 2 a.m. module)

Two columns on Paper; left column deliberately cluttered, right column calm.
Red rule between them.

**Right header (sans, red):** 2 A.M. WITH IT

## Production notes

- Amazon A+ standard modules: use "Standard Company Logo," "Standard Three
  Images & Text," "Standard Comparison Chart" (or image-based equivalent), and
  "Standard Image & Dark Text Overlay" respectively.
- All text lives inside the images where possible for typographic control;
  duplicate the copy in each module's alt-text fields for accessibility and
  SEO — Amazon indexes A+ alt text.
- No employer references anywhere (firewall). Credentials: CDP only.
- Build after the listing is live; A+ publishes only on an active ASIN.
