perform. No stock-photo grief. Rob's face and voice outperform any graphic.

## Smith Publicity — engagement structure

Negotiate one engagement covering three waves:

1. **Wave 1 (Oct–Nov 2026), Toolkit:** Rob as the National Family Caregivers
   Month expert. Target: caregiving press, senior-care trades, NC regional media,
   sandwich-generation podcasts.
2. **Wave 2 (Dec 2026–Feb 2027), Memoir (primary):** long-lead started summer 2026.
   Angles by outlet type: recovery press (the 17-year arc), healthcare trades
   (Studer endorsement, hospice workforce angle), human-interest (the cardinal,
   the date, Tyler & Jaye), adoption outlets (chosen family), faith media
   (signs and meaning, handled honestly).
3. **Wave 3 (Sept–Nov 2027), Children's book:** grief-support and parenting press;
   holiday gift guides (pitched by July); Children's Grief Awareness Day features.

**The evergreen human-interest pitch (all waves):** *A hospice liaison, 17 years
sober, whose father died on Christmas Day — and what landed on his car mirror
15 days later.*
