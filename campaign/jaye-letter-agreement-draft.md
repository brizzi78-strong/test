**AFFILIATE PARTNERSHIP AGREEMENT**

**Between:** Cardinal Promise Press, Raleigh, North Carolina ("Publisher" / Rob Brizzi)
**And:** Jaye Rogers ("Partner")
**Date:** ____________, 2026

Rob and Jaye agree to the following:

**1. What this is.** Jaye will promote the books of Cardinal Promise Press to
her audience in her own voice, on her own schedule. She is an independent
partner, not an employee, and this agreement doesn't restrict anything else
she does.

**2. Which books.** *The Aging Parent Cardinal's Toolkit* (from its release),
*The Cardinal's Promise* (from its release), and future titles only if both
parties add them in writing.

**3. What Jaye earns.** Fifty percent (50%) of the contribution margin on every
tracked sale she drives, defined in dollars per copy in **Exhibit A** (attached
— the per-format table, updated if list prices or printing costs change, with
notice). Tracked sales are: (a) orders on thecardinalspromise.com using her
code or link, and (b) Amazon orders measured through her Amazon Attribution
link. Anything Jaye earns separately from Amazon's own Associates program is
hers alone and outside this agreement.

**4. Payment.** Monthly, by the 10th of the following month, with a statement
showing units, channel, margin, and her share. Publisher issues a 1099.

**5. Disclosure.** Jaye will disclose the paid relationship in her content as
the FTC requires (e.g., "#ad" or "I earn from these links"). Publisher's page
for her carries the same disclosure.

**6. Content.** Jaye speaks in her own words and never needs approval for her
own content. Use of book excerpts, cover art, and the chapter about her family
follows the approvals already agreed for the book itself. Publisher may feature
Jaye's name, handle, and approved quotes on her page and in campaign materials,
and will stop on request.

**7. What this is not.** This agreement is separate from, and is not payment
for, (a) the chapter in *The Cardinal's Promise* about Jaye and Tyler, which
exists only with their approval, and (b) any appearance at launch events, which
are agreed separately.

**8. Term.** Twelve months from signing, renewing automatically. Either party
may end it with 30 days' written notice. Commissions earned before the end date
are still paid.

**9. The simple stuff.** North Carolina law governs. Disputes get a good-faith
conversation before anything else. This page plus Exhibit A is the whole
agreement; changes in writing (email counts).

**Signed:**
