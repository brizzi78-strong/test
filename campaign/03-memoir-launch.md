**Title:** The Cardinal's Promise
**Subtitle:** Immigrant. Addict. Son.
**Imprint:** Cardinal Promise Press
**Formats:** Paperback, ebook, audiobook (author-narrated — the voice IS the brand)
**Team:** Foreword Dave Meyer · Endorsement Quint Studer · Publicity Smith Publicity
· Cover quote Dr. Huet · Book trailer in production
**Assets already live:** thecardinalspromise.com, Kit taxonomy + welcome sequences,
Preorder Buyers database, Amazon Ads playbook

## Launch date and why

**On-sale: Saturday, January 9, 2027.**

Eighteen years sober. One year to the day since the cardinal landed on the mirror.
Two weeks after the first anniversary of Lou's death. No publishing convention
("launch on a Tuesday") outranks this. The date is the marketing.

- **Preorder opens:** October 6, 2026 (Toolkit launch day — back matter goes live
  pointing at a working link)
- **Launch event:** week of January 9 — see The Rollio & Juliet Event below

## The Rollio & Juliet Event (launch centerpiece)

Already logged in Notion as the launch concept — this plan operationalizes it:

**Format:** Live video event (TikTok Live + YouTube simulcast), January 9, 2027.
Tyler Lawrence and Jaye Rogers (@rolliojuliet, ~200K reached) read and react —
live, unrehearsed — to "The Family Afterwards," the chapter about them.

**Run of show (60 min):**
1. Rob tells the 2017 story — meeting Tyler before everything (5 min)
2. Tyler & Jaye read the chapter aloud, trading pages (20 min)
3. Open conversation: what a second chance asks of the people who love you (20 min)
4. Audience Q&A (10 min)
5. The promise, spoken plainly + where to get the book (5 min)

**Mechanics:** advance copies to Tyler & Jaye months prior (approval gate);
co-announcement content on @rolliojuliet in the two weeks before; clip harvest
plan — 8–12 vertical clips scheduled across the following month; Kit signup link
pinned throughout.

**Why it works:** it is not an ad. It is the book's thesis — showing up —
performed live by the people it happened to, in front of the audience that
already loves them.

## Copy blocks

**One-sentence pitch:**
> A redemption story weaving adoption, addiction, recovery, loss, and the calling
> found in end-of-life care — for anyone afraid to walk into a room where someone
> is dying, and for those who do it anyway.

**Jacket blurb (working):**
> Rob's journey runs from a Mexico City adoption to the grip of addiction, into
> hard-won recovery and a life rebuilt through service. When his father Lou dies
> on Christmas Day, Rob knows how to be there. Fifteen days later, on the
> seventeenth anniversary of his sobriety, a cardinal appears. Some moments feel
> like coincidence. Others feel like a promise.

**Subject lines:** "17 years. 15 days. One bird." · "The drive to Cooperstown" ·
"'You hungry?'" · "Why January 9th" · "He kept finding me"
