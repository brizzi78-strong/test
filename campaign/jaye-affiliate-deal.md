**Deal in one line:** Jaye (@rolliojuliet) gets her own affiliate program on
**The Cardinal's Promise only** — 50% of the contribution margin on every
memoir sale she drives, tracked through her own link and code.

Toolkit. It's her family's book — the chapter about her and Tyler is in it —
so her share comes from that title alone. The Toolkit and children's book stay

## Why 50% and why Jaye

- Her chapter ("The Family Afterwards") is in the memoir; the launch event is
  built around her and Tyler. She isn't a hired promoter — she's in the book.
- Her ~200K TikTok reach is the recovery/caregiver audience, exactly the buyer.
- 50% of margin keeps her promoting for months, not one launch weekend. A flat
  fee buys a post; a real split builds a partner.

## What Jaye earns per sale — the honest math

**The hard truth about Amazon:** Amazon doesn't tell us who sent a buyer, and
Amazon Associates (Amazon's own affiliate program, which Jaye can join herself)
pays only ~4.5% on books. A 50% split is only possible on sales **we can see
and track** — which means direct sales, or Amazon sales measured through
Amazon Attribution links during defined windows.

## Mechanics (keep it simple)

1. **Her page:** thecardinalspromise.com/jaye (or /rolliojuliet — page copy
   already drafted in Notion). Her story, both books, her code pre-applied.
2. **Her code:** `JAYE` — 10% off for buyers on direct sales (comes out of the
   gross before the split; the table above already nets it conservatively).
3. **Tracking:** unique code + link on direct store; Amazon Attribution tag for
   Amazon windows; everything visible to her in a shared monthly statement.
4. **Payment:** monthly, by the 10th for the prior month, from Cardinal Promise
   Press. 1099 at year end.
5. **Kit tie-in:** her link tags subscribers `event-rollio` / source=jaye so
   her audience gets credited even when they buy later from an email.

## Agreement checklist (one-page letter agreement, not a 20-page contract)

- [ ] Parties: Cardinal Promise Press / Jaye Rogers
- [ ] Scope: **The Cardinal's Promise only** (other titles only by written addition later)
- [ ] Rate: 50% of contribution margin as defined by the tables above; tables

- [ ] Term: 12 months, auto-renewing, either party out with 30 days' notice;

- [ ] **FTC disclosure:** she must disclose the affiliate relationship in
      content (#ad / "I earn from these links") — protects her, not just us
- [ ] Content approvals: she never needs approval to speak in her own voice;
      chapter excerpts and book assets get approved once, up front
- [ ] The chapter itself remains a separate, prior approval (already required
      before ANY excerpt marketing — this deal doesn't change that)

  is her family's story, told with approval; the affiliate deal is for her
  ongoing promotion work. Keep those two sentences separate and true.
- Not exclusive. She can promote anything else she wants.
