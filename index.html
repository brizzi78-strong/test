Travel & Experiences

Collect moments, not things
  • Stand under the northern lights, somewhere far north and quiet
  • Walk a multi-day trail — Dolomites, Patagonia, or the Kumano Kodo
  • Take one trip with no itinerary and let the days decide
  • Learn a dish from a local in the place it belongs to

Chasing the feeling of awe, space, and the good kind of tired.

💼

Career & Growth

Build things I'm proud of
  • Own one project end to end — start to ship
  • Go deep on a single skill instead of wide on five
  • Twelve real conversations — one a month
  • Ask for the stretch instead of waiting to be handed it

Work that's challenging, meaningful, and leaves room for a life.

📖

Become a Published Author

The dream with my name on the spine
  • Finish the manuscript — a complete first draft, badly written is fine
  • Write most days, even if it's only 300 words before the world wakes up
  • Revise with a real reader's eyes; share pages and survive the feedback
  • Query agents or publish it myself — but get it into readers' hands
  • Hold the finished book. See it on one shelf that isn't mine.

A writer is just someone who didn't quit. The only failure is the unwritten page.

🌱

Health & Wellness

The foundation everything stands on
  • Move four times a week — in a way I actually enjoy
  • Guard sleep like it's the appointment it is
  • Cook more than I order in
  • Ten minutes of stillness, daily and unskippable

Success looks like waking up rested and showing up steady.

❤️

Personal & Relationships

The people and the place I come home to
  • Call the people I love more often than feels efficient
  • Host the gathering instead of waiting for an invitation
  • Make my space feel like mine — light, plants, order
  • Automate the savings; spend the rest on people and experiences

Building a life that's grounded, generous, and unhurried.
