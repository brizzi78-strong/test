Prefer to give in crypto?

If crypto is your thing, you can donate directly to a charity wallet below. To be clear about what this is and isn't:

  • This is a donation, not a purchase. There is no Cardinal coin or token, nothing to buy, and nothing to resell.
  • You receive no asset and expect no profit. It's simply another way to give.
  • Funds go to named charity; we publish the wallet so giving is transparent and verifiable on-chain.
