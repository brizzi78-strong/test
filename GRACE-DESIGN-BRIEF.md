# Design Brief for Grace Nobrega — two covers, one brand

The cardinal is the brand. One bird style (painterly), one type system, one
imprint name across both books. Job 1 is needed now; Job 2 has no deadline.

## Brand rules across both covers
- One imprint name everywhere: CARDINAL PROMISE PRESS

- Same painterly bird style on both books — they must read as siblings on a shelf
- Same serif type family for titles
