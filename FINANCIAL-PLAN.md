## The five moves

### 1 · Deploy the $360K (now)
$50K emergency fund → clear cards → $25K launch reserve → ~$60K Roth-tax
fund → **~$220K to brokerage**, DCA over 9 months into 65% US / 25%
intl / 10% treasuries. → *[investment-plan.md](investment-plan.md)*

### 2 · Run the Roth conversion ladder (every December, 2026–2032)
Convert $60–80K/yr of the $426K traditional IRA, filling the 24% bracket,
staying under the $505K SALT cliff, taxes paid from cash. Ends with the
IRA fully Roth — tax-free forever, no RMDs, backdoor opens after.
**The single highest-value move in the plan.** → *[financial-goals-2026-2031.md](financial-goals-2026-2031.md)*

### 3 · Keep the machine automatic (always)
Zero-based budget (Ledgerly), payroll Roth 401(k) + invested HSA,
$20K/yr giving via appreciated shares, annual December
rebalance-convert-review. → *[ledgerly/](ledgerly/), [tax-planning.md](tax-planning.md)*

### 4 · Manage the house, don't serve it
Catch up Truist + autopay (now). PMI: not worth capital, auto-ends at
78% LTV. Refi to 15-year on trigger: rate ≤ 5.24% or break-even < 24
months; ≤4.5% automatic. Don't race the payoff — the after-tax rate is
~4.4% and the portfolio outearns it.

### 5 · Fund the mission, gated (2026–2027)
The Cardinal's Promise: Toolkit first → adult book → kids book (Rob &
Lou). $25K cap, three-talks gate, eight validation calls, kill criteria.
Deductions follow real revenue and clean books — never precede them.
→ *[launch-plan.md](launch-plan.md)*
