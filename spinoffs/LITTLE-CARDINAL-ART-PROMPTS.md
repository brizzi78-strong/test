# The Little Cardinal's Promise — art generation prompts

Fifteen illustrations. Keep the SAME cast references you used for The Promise:
Grandpa = the Lou character (glasses, gray mustache, green plaid shirt);
Mateo = the dark-haired boy. Style line for every prompt: "Pixar-style warm
storybook illustration, soft painted texture, no text anywhere in the image."
KDP: tick the AI-images disclosure at upload. Square or landscape crops both
work — pages are 8.5x8.5.

1. A boy and his grandpa in a driveway at golden hour, ball gloves on, an old
   faded-red pickup truck behind them.
2. Little-league bleachers; the grandpa sitting alone in the third row, arms
   crossed, proud; the boy waving from the field.
3. A park at golden hour, the grandpa mid-swing with a bat, a baseball high
   against the sky, the boy camped under it with his glove.
4. Extreme close-up of a pinky promise between a big weathered hand and a
   small hand, warm kitchen light.
5. A quiet bedroom, the grandpa resting in a chair with a blanket, the boy
   sitting close; a child's crayon drawings taped to the wall.
6. The boy's hand held in the grandpa's hand on a quilt, their faces close,
   tender, soft lamplight.
7. Evening snowfall on the old red pickup parked in the driveway, one warm
   lit window in the house.
8. WORDLESS: the empty little-league bleachers in winter, snow dusting the
   third row. Wide, still, blue-gray light.
9. A kitchen morning, the boy looking up from a bowl of cereal toward the
   window, alert.
10. KEY IMAGE: a brilliant red cardinal perched on a snowy windowsill seen
    from inside, the boy's amazed face reflected faintly in the glass.
11. The mother kneeling beside the boy at the window, her hand on his
    shoulder, both watching the cardinal, her eyes shining.
12. The boy's small hand pressed flat against the cold windowpane, the
    cardinal on the other side tilting its head — composition echoing the
    pinky promise of image 4.
13. Triptych/montage: the cardinal on a fence by a school bus stop; on the
    backstop at a ballfield; outside a rainy window at dusk.
14. The boy standing in a spring backyard looking up; the cardinal on the
    third branch of a bare tree, exactly where a third-row seat would be.
15. Dawn over a small neighborhood in soft pink light, the cardinal in
    flight above the rooftops.
