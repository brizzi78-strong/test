# Publishing & Distribution Plan — The Cardinal's Promise

Rob owns both ISBNs and publishes as Cardinal Press (Raleigh, NC).
All channels below are nonexclusive and free to enter. The one rule: NEVER
enroll the ebook in KDP Select — its exclusivity clause would forbid every
non-Amazon ebook channel and direct EPUB sales.

## The stack, in launch order

1. **Amazon KDP** — paperback + Kindle. Use own ISBN, imprint Cardinal Promise
   Press. 227pp b/w 6x9 print cost ≈ $3.60; at $19.99 list the royalty is
   ≈ $8.40/copy (60% of list minus print). Kindle at 70% in the $2.99–9.99 band.
   This is the shelf everyone checks and where most sales happen.
2. **Direct on robs site** — signed paperbacks + EPUB. Stock via KDP author
   copies (unlimited, at print cost + shipping). Signed copy at $24.99 nets
   ≈ $18 after processing. EPUB direct nets ~95%. Storefront: Shopify, or
   Payhip/Gumroad for zero setup. This channel powers back-of-room sales at
   talks and bulk orders (treatment centers, hospice orgs, churches, grief
   groups, 20–50 copies at a time).
3. **Google Play Books** — same EPUB, ebook ISBN, 70% of list (US). Same
   partner account enables Google Books search preview — free discovery.
4. **Apple Books** — via Apple Books for Authors (web upload, no Mac needed).
   70% of list flat, no price bands. Second-largest ebook store.
5. **Kobo / B&N Press** — optional; direct or via Draft2Digital (~10% fee for
   one-upload convenience). Add when there's demand.
6. **IngramSpark** — paperback only, later, if bookstores/libraries ask. Same
   ISBN carries over. Expect ~45–55% after wholesale discount.

## Production notes

- Google and Apple validate EPUBs strictly — run EPUBCheck on the final file
  before upload (ask Claude; ten-minute job against the final build).
- KDP: choose "Use my own ISBN"; imprint field = Cardinal Press.
- Barcode: with own ISBN, either supply a barcode on the wrap or let KDP print
  its own bottom-right of the back cover (zone already reserved in the specs).
- Pre-press checklist lives across: KDP-COVER-SPECS.md, EPIGRAPH-PERMISSIONS.md,
  LEGAL-REVIEW.md, BLURB-REQUESTS.md. ISBNs drop into 000-front.md when issued.
