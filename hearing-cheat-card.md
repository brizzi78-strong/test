**Ask the Court for:** $7,500 + IRS interest + court costs. **One line:** *Tesla completed a $7,500 credit form, never filed it, and billed me.*

## BRING (3 copies each) + proof of service on Tesla's agent
**A** Completed Form 15400 (Tesla's own form, $7,500) · **B** IRS CP23 · **C** Order Agreement · **D** Emails to Tesla (Mar/May/Jun) · **E** Form 8936

## SAY FIRST (slow, calm)
"Your Honor, in August 2025 I bought a Tesla Model Y and qualified for the $7,500 EV credit. Tesla completed its own IRS form — Exhibit A — putting my credit at $7,500. But Tesla never filed the matching report with the IRS. So the IRS denied it — Exhibit B — and billed me $7,500 plus interest. I qualified. I did everything right. I'm asking for $7,500 plus interest and costs."
