## 2. SALT × Roth conversion interaction ⚠️

The $40,400 SALT cap **phases down above $505K MAGI** (MFJ 2026), 30¢
per dollar over, floor $10K. Roth conversions raise MAGI:
- Base ~$300K + a $60–80K conversion ≈ $370–380K → safe, full SALT.
- A large conversion year approaching $500K starts clawing back SALT —
  a hidden cost on top of the conversion's own tax.
- **Action:** size each December's conversion to fill the 24% bracket
  AND stay under the ~$505K cliff. Two constraints, one decision.

  15 years (likely the myth's source). Plan: lose early, aim for 3-of-5,
