## The strategic spine

The three books are three doors into the same promise —
*show up for the people who are dying, and for the people who love them:*

- The **Toolkit** serves the family in the middle of it (practical door).
- The **memoir** tells the story of why (emotional door).
- The **children's book** hands the promise to the youngest grievers (legacy door).

Each launch builds the audience for the next. The Toolkit's back matter sells the
memoir. The memoir's platform launches the children's book. Nothing launches cold.

## Ground rules (non-negotiable)

1. **The firewall:** Rob's employer affiliation is never mentioned in any

2. **Credentials used publicly:** CDP (Certified Dementia Practitioner) and
