The Newsletter

A little light in your inbox.

Gentle reflections from The Cardinal's Promise and practical help from the Toolkit — sent at a human pace. No noise, no pressure, unsubscribe any time.

We'll only email you about the books and caregiving resources. Your address is never sold or shared.

What to expect

The welcome series

New subscribers receive a short, paced sequence — a companion for the first few weeks, not a firehose. Edit this outline to match your real emails.

Email 1 · Welcome & the promise

A warm hello, the story behind the cardinal, and a single gentle reflection to sit with. Sent immediately on signup.

Email 2 · You're allowed to rest

For caregivers: permission and one practical step toward respite, with a link to the resource library. Sent day 3.

Email 3 · When grief doesn't follow the rules

On grief without tidy stages, plus a reflection prompt from the Toolkit. Sent day 7.

Email 4 · A small ritual of remembrance

One simple way to honor someone you love, and an invitation to the reader portal. Sent day 12.

Email 5 · You're not alone in this

An invitation to the community, the discussion guides, and ongoing monthly notes. Sent day 18.

After the welcome series, expect roughly one thoughtful note a month — never more than you'd want from a friend who's checking in.

For the site owner

Connecting an email provider

This form is built to drop straight into any email service. To go live:

  1. Create a signup form / audience in your provider (e.g. Kit, Mailchimp, Buttondown).
  2. Copy its form action URL into the action="" attribute of the form above. The fields email, first_name, and interest are already named for easy mapping.
  3. Recreate the welcome series above as an automated sequence in that provider.

Until an action URL is set, the form stays fully functional and shows a friendly confirmation message instead of failing — so the page is never broken in testing.
