I'm seeking representation (or a consultation) on a consumer claim against Tesla,

records, and has not corrected the filing despite written demands.

I believe this supports a North Carolina UDTPA claim (§ 75-1.1) for treble damages
and attorney's fees, and possibly negligent misrepresentation. The same failure is
hitting other EV buyers (Tesla and Rivian), so a class may be appropriate.
