# The Cardinal's Promise — Toolkit Materials

Companion materials for *Aging Parent Cardinal's Toolkit* (Cardinal's Promise
Series, Rob Brizzi), the North Carolina family caregiver handbook.

## Contents

- `trifold/trifold-panels.md` — panel-by-panel copy and fold map for the
  laminated trifold **Crisis Companion**: a quick-reference distillation of
  the handbook's Quick Start Guide (first 24–72 hours of a caregiving
  crisis), not a mini-book.
- `trifold/trifold-print.html` — print-ready layout (US Letter landscape,
  two-sided, roll fold). Open in a browser and print to PDF at 100% scale,
  flip on short edge. Dashed fold guides are on-screen aids — remove the
  `border-right` rule on `.panel` for the final press run. Replace the QR
  placeholder with a real code pointing to thecardinalspromise.com.

## Design intent

The trifold is the bird's-eye map; the full toolkit is the deep woods guide.
Every line on it answers "what do I do right now?" — checkable boxes, the
handful of North Carolina phone numbers that open every other door, and the
book's most crisis-critical warnings (inpatient vs. observation status;
sudden confusion is often reversible; see an elder-law attorney before
moving money).
