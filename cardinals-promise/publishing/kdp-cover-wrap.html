Caring for an aging parent is overwhelming — nobody hands you a map.

Color-coded by urgency — Act Today, Watch Closely, Plan Ahead — The Caregiver's Cardinal Toolkit helps your family get organized, make informed decisions, and face the healthcare system with confidence, even on your worst day.

Inside you'll find practical tools to help you:

  • Organize medical, legal, and financial information
  • Prepare for emergencies and care transitions
  • Compare care options — from home health to hospice — and how to pay
  • Track appointments, medications, and important contacts
  • Protect yourself from burnout — and avoid the mistakes that cost families money

Rob Brizzi is a Certified Dementia Practitioner with a career across home health, hospice, and senior living. His work at the bedside of patients and families has shaped his mission to bring comfort, grace, and dignity to the end-of-life journey. Rob lives in North Carolina with his wife, Hope Brizzi, PharmD.
