### Targeting notes
- Best-fit firms: elder law, estate planning, special-needs planning; also
  financial advisors and fiduciaries (adapt the wording).
- Find them: your state's **NAELA** chapter (naela.org), county bar elder-law
  sections, and local estate-planning councils.
- Timing: attorneys plan marketing budgets around year-end and Q1 — good windows.
- Compliance: let the firm handle its own state-bar advertising rules; your side
  is just a bulk book sale + a neutral insert.
