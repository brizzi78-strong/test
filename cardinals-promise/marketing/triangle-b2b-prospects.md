### Outreach order
1. **Elder-law attorneys** — warmest fit; they already tell families to plan early.
2. **Aging Life Care managers** — they place resources with every family they serve.
3. **Senior centers & support groups** — resource tables + a free talk (also feed the email funnel).
4. **Financial advisors** — client leave-behind for retirement/legacy conversations.
5. **Employers** — bigger, slower, but large volume once landed.

*Reminder: contact details change — confirm before sending. This list is a
starting point, not a vetted endorsement of any firm.*
