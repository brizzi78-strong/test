Questions

Good to know

What’s actually inside the Toolkit?
Two guided workbooks (one for the aging parent, one for the caregiver) plus the illustrated story The Cardinal’s Promise. You’ll find checklists, conversation starters, document trackers, and gentle prompts for each stage of care.
Who is it for?
Any family caring for an aging parent — whether you’re just noticing changes, deep in daily caregiving, or walking the hospice road. It meets you wherever you are.
I’m a care professional — can I use it with families?
Absolutely. Hospices, senior-living communities, and discharge teams use the Toolkit as an intake resource. Ask about partner and bulk options when you request a copy.
What formats does it come in?
A printed workbook set and a digital edition you can fill in on any device. The printed set is the favorite — families like keeping it on the kitchen table.
